Visa & Document Preparation

Navigate the visa process with confidence. Our AI prepares a personalised checklist based on your country and program.

Step 1: Offer Letter

Once you receive your offer from an institution, you will need to confirm your acceptance and pay any initial deposits.

Step 2: AI Checklist

Our AI analyses your profile and generates a country-specific list of documents required for your visa application.

Step 3: Appointment Booking

We help schedule your embassy appointment and prepare you for the interview. You can track your progress online.

Step 4: Arrival & Residence

After approval, we assist with arrival logistics, including accommodation, SIM cards and orientation sessions.
